How does this work?

Scrobbyl uses the Echoprint open source fingerprinter to identify what songs are playing around you. It uses a small flash applet to record, using your computer's microphone. Lookups are performed using the Echo Nest.

It says it doesn't know what's playing

The Echoprint service currently has a small database for testing. Try limiting searches to popular music released before 2003.
